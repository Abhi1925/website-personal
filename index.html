About Me

Sharing a little bit of my story...

My interests include Full Stack Development, Cloud Computing and Machine Learning. Outside of these, my interests are eating different kinds of food, working-out, binge watching Netflix or playing on my PlayStation. You guys can also find me struggling at Ice-Skating, playing Among Us or simply sleeping.
